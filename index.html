Who He Is

Hunter "El Casador" Anderton is a young bachelor, age 16, and a man who enjoys the finer things in life. He loves to hunt(no pun intended), play football, and take long walks on the beach by moonlight.

Hobbies

On a nice afternoon, one may find Hunter in solitude, helping himself toa good book, doing laundry, or watching Ghostbusters. He is also a D&D blackbelt, a master in the arts of hula dancing, and has a degree in Barbie Doll Fashion. However, at night you will most likely find him fighting crime, taking down the mafia, and ultimate punching the eff out of bad guys.

Origin Story

At the age of 10, Hunter loved going to playgrounds and adventuring in the woods. One day, he adventured too far and ran into a hunter's hut. This man bit him and gave him the powers of a hunter, and ever since then, "El casador" has been roaming the streets of Lehi, fighting criminals and small-time thugs.

Favorite Food

Some of his favorite foods include pasta, pizza and hamburgers. However, after a tough fight, El Casador loves sneaking danish pastries from the danish pastry jar.
